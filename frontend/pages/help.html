Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
You can track your order in real-time by clicking on 'Track Order' in the app or website. You'll see your delivery partner's location and estimated arrival time.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, Paytm, Google Pay, PhonePe, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for select restaurants.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel your order before it's prepared by the restaurant. Go to your order history and click 'Cancel' on the order. Full refund will be processed instantly.
How do I get a refund?
Refunds are automatically processed for cancelled orders. For other cases, contact our support team. Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to reflect in your account.
